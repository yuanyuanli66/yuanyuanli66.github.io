Bio

Yuanyuan Li, Ph.D., has joined the Biostatiscs \& Computational Biology Branch at the NIEHS/NIH in May 2011 as a postdoctorial IRTA (Intramural Research Training Award) fellow and is currently a research fellow under the supervision of Dr. Leping Li, P.I. Her primary research interest lies in computational genomics especially cancer genomics. She obtained a Ph.D. from the electrical engineering and computer science department at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville in 2010. Her research interests from artificial intelligence area include, probablistic suffix trees, KD-trees, classification and regression trees, and gradient boosting machines.
